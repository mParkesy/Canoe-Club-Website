Events

Its Go Canoeing Week on the 9th of November. If you would like to register than please go to the Join Us page of our website and fill in the form. Please state in the previous canoeing/kayaking experience box that you would like to join the Go Canoeing session which is on the 14 of November at 10am.

The calendar below includes any external events/competitions in which club members may be interested in. The club does not send a team to every event, but sometimes small groups do attend out of region races, training days or national events.

If you have any questions or would like to contact us then please email: info@canarycanoes.org.uk
