Qualifications

We have two coaches who are able to award canoeing/kayaking qualifications. On the third Sunday of every month, one of these two coaches will take out a small group of new paddlers who wish to get their British Canoeing 1 star award. The other coach will take out anyone who has passed their 1 star award and is aiming for either 2 or 3 star.
There are the occasional foundation safety and rescue courses and first aid courses that either occur at the club or local clubs in the area. If these are available then anyone on our mailing list will be notified.

If you would like to become a coach for our canoe club then you will have to take part in a coaching course run by British Canoeing. These courses can be fairly expensive and run over a few days. They are usually run region by region and different canoe clubs. Coaching awards are split into levels with the Level 1 allowing you to coach beginner adults and Level 2 allowing you to coach beginner children.

If you have any questions or would like to contact us then please email: info@canarycanoes.org.uk
